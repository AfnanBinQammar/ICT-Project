About Us

Behtar Mustaqbil is a non-governmental organization dedicated to creating positive social change by empowering underprivileged communities. It is one of the top NGOs in Pakistan that is dedicated to empowering communities and transforming lives since 1979. From healthcare, education, food security, livelihood, water and sanitation to resilient infrastructure and social development, we tackle development issues at its core.
You can also contribute to our cause by donating Zakat online in Pakistan. Your Zakat can make a significant impact in providing essential services to those in need and help us in our mission to build a better Pakistan where everyone reaches their full potential.
Explore our impact, discover how you can join us, and be part of something bigger. Together, we can build a Pakistan where everyone reaches their full potentialOur mission is to provide access to education, healthcare, and basic resources for those in need, with a special focus on youth development and women’s empowerment.
